Instructions Before the Tasks:

In the following tasks, you will be presented with short scenarios involving an AI system.

  • First, you will see a brief scenario description, prompt made to an AI and an AI recommendation.
  • You will be asked to evalue the AI’s recommendation and decide whether to accept or reject it.
  • Next, you will be shown an explanation of the rationale behind the AI recommendation that describes the reasoning behind the recommendation.
  • You will then have a chance to revisit your original decision based on this additional information.

Please read each scenario and explanation carefully and respond based on your honest evaluation. There are no right or wrong answers.
